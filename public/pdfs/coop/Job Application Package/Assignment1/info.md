## Instructions | 作业说明

### Assignment Instructions: | 作业说明：

1. **Create a Resume following the resume format recommended in GEP1001.**
   根据 GEP1001 推荐的简历格式创建简历。

2. **Follow the Vmock Resume Review Activity guidelines to review, score and improve your resume. Take a screenshot of your Vmock Resume Score Summary page.**
   遵循 Vmock 简历审查活动指南来审查、评分和改进您的简历。截取您的 Vmock 简历评分摘要页面截图。

3. **Compose a cover letter customized to a sample job posting for your program to accompany your resume.**
   撰写一封针对您专业的示例职位发布的定制求职信，与您的简历一起提交。

4. **Combine your documents into one file using PDF format and submit them for review as your Job Application Package assignment. The following documents are required for this assignment:**

   - 将您的文件合并为一个 PDF 格式的文件并作为您的求职申请包作业提交以供审查。此作业需要以下文件：

   - **Resume** | 简历
   - **Vmock Resume Score Summary screenshot** | Vmock 简历评分摘要截图
   - **Cover letter** | 求职信

5. **.ZIP or .RAR file formats cannot be evaluated in Brightspace**
   .ZIP 或.RAR 文件格式无法在 Brightspace 中进行评估

6. **Please Note: You are required to submit both your resume and cover letter for this assignment, and your resume must be assessed as 'Meets Co-op and Career Standards' to gain access to co-op jobs through the Algonquin College online portal, HireAC.**
   请注意：您需要为此作业提交简历和求职信，并且您的简历必须被评估为"符合合作教育和职业标准"，才能通过 Algonquin 学院在线门户 HireAC 访问合作教育工作机会。

7. **You should never copy the content of another resume to use in your resume. Always include accurate and your own resume content! Please view the Authentic Job Searching video for more information.**
   您绝不应复制其他简历的内容用于您的简历。始终包含准确和您自己的简历内容！请观看"真实求职"视频了解更多信息。
