Assignment Instructions:

Create a Resume following the resume format recommended in GEP1001.

Compose a cover letter customized to a sample job posting for your program to accompany your resume.
Combine your documents into one file using PDF format and submit them for review as your Job Application Package assignment. The following documents are required for this assignment:
Resume
Vmock Resume Score Summary screenshot
Cover letter
.ZIP or .RAR file formats cannot be evaluated in Brightspace
Please Note: You are required to submit both your resume and cover letter for this assignment, and your resume must be assessed as 'Meets Co-op and Career Standards' to gain access to co-op jobs through the Algonquin College online portal, HireAC.

You should never copy the content of another resume to use in your resume. Always include accurate and your own resume content! Please view the Authentic Job Searching video for more information.
