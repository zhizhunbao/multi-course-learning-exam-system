# Cover Letter Writing Guide | 求职信写作指南

## Overview | 概述

A well-written cover letter is essential for your Job Application Package. It should be **customized** to a specific job posting and highlight how your skills and experience align with the position requirements.

一封写好的求职信对您的求职申请包至关重要。它应该针对**特定职位发布**定制，并突出说明您的技能和经验如何与该职位要求相匹配。

---

## Standard Cover Letter Format | 标准求职信格式

### 1. Header | 页眉

```
[Your Name]
[Phone Number] | [Email Address]
[LinkedIn Profile] | [GitHub Profile]

---

Date: [Current Date]

[Hiring Manager Name] or "Hiring Manager"
[Company Name]
[Address]

Re: [Job Title] - [Term/Season]
```

### 2. Opening Paragraph | 开头段落

**Purpose**: Introduce yourself and express interest

**要点**：

- Use professional greeting
- State the position you're applying for
- Mention where you saw the posting
- Express enthusiasm

**Example**:

> "I am writing to express my strong interest in the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. As a graduate student pursuing [Your Program] at Algonquin College with [X] years of experience in [Relevant Field], I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to [Company's Mission/Goals]."

### 3. Body Paragraphs (2-3 paragraphs) | 主体段落（2-3 段）

**Paragraph 1: Alignment with Requirements**
**Purpose**: Match your skills to job requirements

- List 3-4 key requirements from the job posting
- Connect your experience to each requirement
- Use specific examples and quantified achievements

**Paragraph 2: Relevant Technical Expertise**
**Purpose**: Showcase your technical skills

- Highlight 2-3 most relevant projects or experiences
- Mention specific technologies, tools, or methodologies
- Connect your academic work to professional needs
- Demonstrate hands-on experience

**Paragraph 3: Cultural Fit & Growth Mindset** (Optional)
**Purpose**: Show you understand the company culture

- Research the company's values, mission, or recent projects
- Explain why you're interested in working there
- Mention soft skills (teamwork, communication, problem-solving)
- Express willingness to learn and grow

### 4. Closing Paragraph | 结尾段落

**Purpose**: Thank, reiterate interest, and request action

**要点**：

- Thank the reader for considering your application
- Briefly restate your enthusiasm
- Request an interview opportunity
- Be professional and confident

**Example**:

> "Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how my background in [Key Skills] can contribute to the success of [Company Name]."

### 5. Signature | 签名

```
Sincerely,

[Your Name]

---

Word Count: [Usually 250-400 words for co-op]
```

## Key Writing Tips | 关键写作技巧

### ✅ DO | 要做

1. **Customize for Each Position**

   - Read the job posting carefully
   - Match keywords from the posting
   - Address specific requirements

2. **Be Specific**

   - Use quantified achievements (metrics, percentages, numbers)
   - Mention specific technologies and tools
   - Give concrete examples from your experience

3. **Show, Don't Tell**

   - Instead of: "I am good at Python"
   - Better: "I architected a FastAPI backend with 88.8% test coverage using pytest"

4. **Keep it Concise**

   - One page maximum
   - 250-400 words for co-op positions
   - Use clear, professional language

5. **Proofread Carefully**
   - Check grammar and spelling
   - Ensure consistency in formatting
   - Read aloud to catch errors

### ❌ DON'T | 不要做

1. **Don't Use Generic Templates**

   - Avoid "To Whom It May Concern"
   - Don't copy-paste template language

2. **Don't Repeat Your Resume**

   - Expand on experiences, don't just list them
   - Tell the story behind your achievements

3. **Don't Focus Only on Yourself**

   - Connect your skills to company needs
   - Show value you'll bring

4. **Don't Be Too Casual**

   - Use professional language
   - Avoid slang, emojis, or humor

5. **Don't Make It Too Long**
   - Respect the reader's time
   - Get to the point quickly

## Language Guidelines | 语言指南

### Professional Phrases | 专业短语

**Expressing Interest**:

- "I am writing to express my strong interest in..."
- "I am enthusiastic about the opportunity to..."
- "I am excited to contribute to..."

**Highlighting Experience**:

- "Throughout my career as [Position], I [achievement]..."
- "In my current studies at Algonquin College, I have..."
- "My experience in [field] has prepared me to..."

**Showing Value**:

- "I am eager to bring my [skills] to..."
- "I look forward to contributing to the success of..."
- "My background in [area] positions me well to..."

**Closing**:

- "Thank you for considering my application."
- "I look forward to discussing this opportunity further."
- "I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to..."

## Formatting Checklist | 格式检查清单

- [ ] One page maximum
- [ ] Professional header with contact information
- [ ] Correct date
- [ ] Proper greeting (no "To Whom It May Concern")
- [ ] Paragraphs are well-structured
- [ ] Professional closing and signature
- [ ] Word count: 250-400 words
- [ ] No grammar or spelling errors
- [ ] Consistent formatting (bullets, spacing, fonts)
- [ ] Saved as PDF for submission
