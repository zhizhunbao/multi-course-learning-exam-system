## 第 3 页

What is Time Boxing?
什么是时间盒？

- A timebox is a fixed period of time when a person or a team
- 时间盒是指一个人或团队

works towards an agreed goal
为实现商定目标而工作的一段固定时间

- Every event in scrum is time boxed
- Scrum 中的每个事件都有时间盒限制
- Sprint - Between 1 and 4 weeks
- 冲刺 - 1 到 4 周之间
- Sprint Planning - 2 hours for each week in a Sprint
- 冲刺规划 - Sprint 中每周 2 小时
- Daily Scrum - 15 minutes
- 每日 Scrum - 15 分钟
- Sprint Review - 2 to 4 hours
- 冲刺评审 - 2 到 4 小时
- Sprint Retrospective - 60 to 90 minutes
- 冲刺回顾 - 60 到 90 分钟

Duration of Sprint Planning Meeting
冲刺规划会议的持续时间

- Sprint planning meetings are Time Boxed events
- 冲刺规划会议是有时间盒限制的事件
- Rule of Thumb for duration:
- 持续时间的经验法则：

Number of Weeks _ 2 Hours
周数 _ 2 小时

- Example:
- 示例：

– A three week sprint should have a maximum 6 hour sprint plan meeting – A two week sprint should have a maximum 4 hour sprint plan meeting
– 三周冲刺应该最多有 6 小时冲刺规划会议 – 两周冲刺应该最多有 4 小时冲刺规划会议

## 第 6 页

Velocity Metric
速度指标

- Measures the amount of work a team can deliver during a Sprint
- 测量团队在冲刺期间可以交付的工作量
- It is used during Sprint Planning to decide the Sprint Backlog
- 在冲刺规划期间用于决定冲刺待办事项
- How to compute Velocity?
- 如何计算速度？

– Assign a number of story points to each story – Compute total story points for all completed stories in the previous 3 sprints
– 为每个故事分配故事点数 – 计算前 3 个冲刺中所有已完成故事的总故事点数
– Velocity is the average story points completed in the last 3 sprints
– 速度是最后 3 个冲刺中完成的故事点数的平均值

## 第 11 页

Sprint Planning Meeting
冲刺规划会议

- Phase 1: Product Owner communicates
- 阶段 1：产品负责人沟通

– Sprint priorities for the team – Overview of sprint objectives – Proposed velocity – Agreement is reached between Product Owner and Team
– 团队的冲刺优先级 – 冲刺目标概述 – 提议的速度 – 产品负责人和团队达成协议

- Phase 2: Team plans how to achieve the committed Sprint goal
- 阶段 2：团队计划如何实现承诺的冲刺目标

– Breakdown stories into further subtasks – Establish deadlines – Prioritize them – Assign to different members
– 将故事分解为更多子任务 – 建立截止日期 – 确定优先级 – 分配给不同成员

## 第 12 页

Daily Scrum Meetings
每日 Scrum 会议

- Short, Time-Boxed (15 Minutes) Meeting
- 短暂、有时间盒限制（15 分钟）的会议
- Team members provide updates on:
- 团队成员提供以下更新：

– What they worked yesterday – What they are planning to work on today – Any impediments or issues for their work to progress
– 昨天做了什么 – 今天计划做什么 – 任何阻碍或影响工作进展的问题
