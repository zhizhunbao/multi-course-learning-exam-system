## 第 2 页

Team Formation and Dynamics All teams goes through stages of development
团队形成与动态 所有团队都经历发展阶段
For teams to be high performing, they must understand the dynamics of team formation
对于高效团队，他们必须理解团队形成的动态
Tuckman's Framework (Bruce W Tuckman 1960s) is very useful for this
Tuckman 框架（Bruce W Tuckman 1960 年代）对此非常有用
Four stages of development:
四个发展阶段：
Forming Storming Norming Performing
形成期 震荡期 规范期 执行期

---

## 第 3 页

Stage 1 - Forming Feelings:
阶段 1 - 形成期情感：
Excited to be part of the team Eager about the work ahead
为成为团队一员而兴奋 对未来的工作充满期待
High positive expectations Anxiety about team fitment and performance
高度积极的期望 对团队适应性和表现的焦虑

---

## 第 4 页

Stage 1 - Forming Behaviors:
阶段 1 - 形成期行为：
Lots of questions from team members about About the new team
团队成员提出大量问题 关于新团队
About their role and fitment
关于他们的角色和适应性

---

## 第 5 页

Stage 2 - Storming Feelings:
阶段 2 - 震荡期情感：
End of early excitement and expectations Frustration/Anger about team's processes/progress
早期兴奋和期望的结束 对团队过程和进展的挫败感/愤怒
Concerns about not meeting team's goals
对无法达成团队目标的担忧

---

## 第 6 页

Stage 2 - Storming Behaviors:
阶段 2 - 震荡期行为：
Less polite interactions Frequent disagreements and arguments about goals and solution approach
礼貌互动减少 关于目标和解决方案的频繁分歧和争论
Lack of trust among members
成员之间缺乏信任

---

## 第 7 页

Stage 2 - Storming Team Tasks:
阶段 2 - 震荡期团队任务：
Refocus on goals Breakdown large goals into smaller achievable ones
重新聚焦目标 将大目标分解为更小的可实现目标
Try to re-define the goals to avoid confusion
尝试重新定义目标以避免混淆

---

## 第 8 页

Stage 3 - Norming Feelings:
阶段 3 - 规范期情感：
Less discrepancy between individual and team expectations
个人期望与团队期望之间的差异减少
Increased sense of comfort in expressing their ideas and feelings to the team
在向团队表达想法和感受时舒适感增加
More willingness to accept view points of others in the team
更愿意接受团队中其他人的观点
Constructive criticism is both possible and welcomed
建设性批评既可能又受欢迎

---

## 第 9 页

Stage 3 - Norming Behaviors:
阶段 3 - 规范期行为：
Members making conscious effort to resolve problems and achieve group harmony
成员有意识地努力解决问题并实现团队和谐
More frequent and meaningful communication among team members
团队成员之间更频繁和有意义的沟通
Increased willingness to share ideas or ask teammates for help
更愿意分享想法或向队友寻求帮助
Members return their focus to team tasks and follow established team rules and processes
成员重新聚焦团队任务并遵循既定的团队规则和流程

---

## 第 10 页

Stage 3 - Norming Team Tasks:
阶段 3 - 规范期团队任务：
Increase in productivity, both team individual level and team level
生产力提高，无论是个人层面还是团队层面
Evaluation of team processes and productivity
评估团队流程和生产力

---

## 第 11 页

Stage 4 - Performing Feelings:
阶段 4 - 执行期情感：
Members feel satisfaction in team's progress Aware of own's and others strength and weaknesses
成员对团队的进展感到满意 意识到自己和他人 strengths 和 weaknesses
Feel attached to the team Feel confident in their individual abilities and that of teammates
感到与团队紧密相连 对自己的个人能力以及队友的能力充满信心

---

## 第 12 页

Stage 4 - Performing Behaviors:
阶段 4 - 执行期行为：
Able to solve problems together Roles of the members become more fluid
能够共同解决问题 成员的角色变得更加灵活
Differences among members are appreciated and leveraged for the team performance
成员之间的差异受到赞赏并用于提升团队表现

---

## 第 13 页

Stage 4 - Performing Team Tasks:
阶段 4 - 执行期团队任务：
Make significant progress towards team goals Continue to deepen their knowledge and skills
朝着团队目标取得重大进展 继续深化知识和技能
Celebrate team accomplishments
庆祝团队成就
